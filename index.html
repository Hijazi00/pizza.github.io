Delicious and Easy Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough:
    • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Pizza Sauce:
    • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • ½ teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparing the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Gradually add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Making the Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  3. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Remove from heat and set aside.

Assembling and Baking the Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a generous amount of the prepared sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, such as shredded mozzarella cheese, sliced bell peppers, pepperoni, and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
